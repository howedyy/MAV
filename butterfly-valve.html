Butterfly Valves

Compact design for large diameter flow control

Our butterfly valves offer cost-effective flow control for large diameter applications. Available in wafer, lug, and flanged configurations with manual, electric, and pneumatic actuation options, manufactured to GB and DIN standards.

GB/DIN Standard

Technical Specifications

Size Range

  • DN 50 - DN 1200 (2" - 48")
  • Large diameter specialization
  • Custom sizes available

Pressure Rating

  • Class 150 - 600 LB
  • 16 - 25 bar (DIN Standard)
  • Vacuum to full pressure

Temperature Range

  • -10°C to 120°C (14°F to 248°F)
  • High temperature versions available
  • EPDM, Viton, PTFE seals

Materials

  • Body: Cast Iron, Ductile Iron
  • Disc: Ductile Iron, Stainless Steel
  • Seat: EPDM, NBR, Viton
  • Shaft: Stainless Steel 316

Design Types

  • Wafer Type (Between flanges)
  • Lug Type (Individual bolting)
  • Flanged Type (ANSI/DIN flanges)
  • Grooved End Type

Standards

  • GB/T 12238 (China Standard)
  • DIN 3354 (German Standard)
  • API 609/6D compliance
  • MSS SP-67/68 certified

Key Features

Compact lightweight design
Quarter-turn operation
Low installation cost
Bubble-tight sealing
Multiple actuation options
Low maintenance requirements

Applications

Water Treatment

Municipal water treatment plants, distribution systems, and sewage applications.

HVAC Systems

Chilled water, hot water, and air handling systems in commercial buildings.

Industrial Process

Chemical plants, food processing, and pharmaceutical manufacturing.

Power Generation

Cooling water systems, intake and discharge applications in power plants.

Marine Applications

Ballast water systems, seawater cooling, and shipboard applications.

Irrigation & Agriculture

Large diameter irrigation systems and agricultural water management.
