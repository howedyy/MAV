Check Valves

Automatic backflow prevention for system protection

Our check valves provide automatic backflow prevention with minimal pressure drop. Available in swing, lift, and tilting disc designs, manufactured to GB and DIN standards for reliable operation in water, oil, gas, and steam applications.

GB/DIN Standard

Technical Specifications

Size Range

  • DN 15 - DN 600 (1/2" - 24")
  • Wafer and flanged designs
  • Compact installation dimensions

Pressure Rating

  • Class 150 - 1500 LB
  • 16 - 100 bar (DIN Standard)
  • Low pressure drop design

Temperature Range

  • -29°C to 425°C (-20°F to 800°F)
  • Steam service applications
  • Cryogenic options available

Materials

  • Cast Iron: GG25, GGG40, GGG50
  • Cast Steel: WCB, WCC, WC6, WC9
  • Stainless Steel: CF8, CF8M, CF3M
  • Bronze and Special Alloys

Design Types

  • Swing Check Valves
  • Lift Check Valves
  • Tilting Disc Check Valves
  • Ball Check Valves
  • Dual Plate Check Valves

Standards

  • GB/T 12236 (China Standard)
  • DIN 3202 (German Standard)
  • API 594/6D compliance
  • ISO 14313 certified

Key Features

Automatic operation
Minimal pressure drop
Fast closure response
Low water hammer
Horizontal/vertical installation
Maintenance-free operation

Applications

Water Systems

Municipal water distribution, booster pumps, and water treatment facilities.

Steam Applications

Steam lines, boiler feedwater systems, and condensate return lines.

Oil & Gas

Pipeline protection, pump discharge lines, and refinery applications.

Chemical Processing

Chemical plant piping systems and process equipment protection.

Power Generation

Cooling water systems, steam turbine applications, and auxiliary systems.

Irrigation Systems

Agricultural irrigation, sprinkler systems, and water distribution networks.
