Strainer Valves

Mechanical filtration for system protection

Our strainer valves provide mechanical filtration to protect downstream equipment from debris and particulates. Available in Y-type and basket configurations with easy maintenance features, manufactured to DIN standards for reliable operation.

DIN Standard

Technical Specifications

Size Range

  • DN 15 - DN 400 (1/2" - 16")
  • Y-type and basket strainers
  • Compact and standard designs

Pressure Rating

  • Class 150 - 600 LB
  • 16 - 40 bar (DIN Standard)
  • Full pressure differential

Temperature Range

  • -20°C to 200°C (-4°F to 392°F)
  • Steam service applications
  • High temperature options

Materials

  • Body: Cast Iron, Ductile Iron
  • Screen: Stainless Steel 304/316
  • Cover: Cast Iron, Steel
  • Bolting: Carbon Steel, SS

Screen Options

  • Perforated screens (1.6-25mm)
  • Mesh screens (10-200 mesh)
  • Wedge wire screens
  • Custom perforation patterns

Standards

  • DIN 3202 (German Standard)
  • GB/T 19672 (China Standard)
  • ASME B16.34 compliance
  • ISO 5208 testing

Key Features

Easy maintenance access
Low pressure drop
High debris capacity
Removable screen element
Drain plug provision
Multiple screen materials

Applications

Industrial Process

Protection of pumps, compressors, and control valves from debris and particulates.

Steam Systems

Steam line filtration and protection of steam traps and control equipment.

Water Treatment

Municipal water systems, cooling water, and industrial water applications.

Power Generation

Boiler feedwater systems, cooling circuits, and auxiliary system protection.

Chemical Processing

Chemical plant piping protection and process equipment filtration.

Food & Beverage

Sanitary straining applications in food processing and beverage production.
