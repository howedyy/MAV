Globe Valves

Precise flow control for critical applications

Our globe valves excel in throttling and flow regulation applications, providing accurate control with excellent sealing capability. Built to GB and DIN standards, these valves offer superior performance in steam, water, oil, and chemical service applications.

GB/DIN Standard

Technical Specifications

Size Range

  • DN 15 - DN 500 (1/2" - 20")
  • Standard and custom sizes available
  • Compact design options

Pressure Rating

  • Class 150 - 2500 LB
  • 16 - 160 bar (DIN Standard)
  • High pressure designs available

Temperature Range

  • -46°C to 540°C (-50°F to 1000°F)
  • Cryogenic applications available
  • High temperature steam service

Materials

  • Cast Iron: GG25, GGG40, GGG50
  • Cast Steel: WCB, WCC, WC6, WC9, WC12
  • Stainless Steel: CF8, CF8M, CF3M, CF3
  • Bronze and Special Alloys

End Connections

  • Flanged (RF, FF, RTJ)
  • Butt Weld (BW)
  • Socket Weld (SW)
  • Threaded (NPT, BSP)
  • Union End connections

Standards

  • GB/T 12235 (China Standard)
  • DIN 3356 (German Standard)
  • API 602/623 compliance
  • ASME B16.34 certified

Key Features

Excellent throttling control
Superior sealing performance
Linear flow characteristic
Multi-turn operation
Angle and straight patterns
Low maintenance requirements

Applications

Steam Systems

Superheated and saturated steam applications in power plants and industrial facilities.

Chemical Processing

Flow control in chemical reactors, distillation columns, and process equipment.

HVAC Control

Temperature and flow regulation in heating and cooling systems.

Oil & Gas

Flow regulation in refineries, gas processing plants, and pipeline systems.

Water Treatment

Flow control in water treatment plants and distribution systems.

Pharmaceutical

Precise flow control in pharmaceutical manufacturing and clean room applications.
